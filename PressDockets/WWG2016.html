Colorado Community Radio Network is the largest grouping of independent and community-oriented radio stations in Colorado. We handle a potential listening audience of about 500,000 and our listening range is growing throughout the state. All the stations involved in the network will be involved in the promotions negotiated for this event.

We are always looking to be involved in all aspects of promoting our community and its multitude of talents and ideas. This allows us to earn a dedicated and passionate listener base. We continually work to grow that fan base and work with organizations such as yours, so we can provide a solid growth opportunity for everyone involved. Our rates are always trade negotiable and always lower than any other stations with the same potential listener base.

We are very interested in working with your organization to help promote your event. We would love to discuss options for us as a station to be considered as a viable media outlet for your event, to have the option of helping promote or be involved in this event in any way possible. Thank you for your time and consideration. We look forward to hearing back from you.
